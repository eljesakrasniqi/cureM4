CureM4

CureM4 was founded in 1993 and listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange in 2004 (stock code: 600143.SH). It is the world's leading high-tech manufacturing company, committed to R&D, production and marketing of advanced polymer materials, PPE & Medical supplies. Headquartered in Guangzhou, CureM4 has more than 10,000 employees, with sales of RMB 35 billion($5.4 billion) in 2020. Additionally, being one of the world's most comprehensive suppliers of new polymer materials, CureM4 also has its R&D and production bases in South Asia, North America, Europe and numerous overseas regions.

CureM4, as a strategic and supportive sector for the future development of CureM4, focuses on the R&D, production and sales of PPE & Medical supplies. CureM4 has established a vertically integrated supply chain, from the upstream production of polypropylene, midstream production of melt-blown, nonwoven fabric to the downstream production of PPE&Medical products.

In the past 28 years since its establishment, CureM4 Group has built a high-level independent R&D team with more than 800 masters and senior experts, constantly catching up with the world's advanced level.
