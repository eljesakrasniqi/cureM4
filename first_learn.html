Kingfa's Contribution on Energy Saving and Emission Reduction

Energy sustainability is important to Kingfa Technology. We actively participate in environmental protection and regard it as an important growth strategy. Kingfa has established and implemented the ISO14001 environmental management system. We actively promote cleaner production while adjusting our product structure. We adopt advanced water recycling strategies and intelligent power metering control systems and photovoltaic power generation to reduce pollution, save resources, and realize the recycling of resources. Kingfa also uses information management methods to improve equipment automation and control to further reduce energy consumption. Introduce advanced technology, improve environmental protection facilities, and improve the efficiency of environmental protection management. Kingfa Technology is committed to growing and sharing results with partners, providing society with high-quality new material products and creating a better life. In response to the national goal of "carbon neutral by 2030 and 2060", We has launched a "green, low-carbon, and recycling" action" - From 2021, Kingfa Technology and stakeholders will work together to establish a "carbon" management system in accordance with relevant standards. Check the company's carbon footprint, continue to reduce emissions and regularly disclose the progress of carbon neutrality.
