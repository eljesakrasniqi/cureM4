Employee Training and Career Promotion Programs

Kingfa Science & Technology officially established the School of Management in 2014, marking a new milestone in the work of cultivating the Kingfa Science & Technology personnel. The School of Management has created a comprehensive system of training and development for its staff. Through a series of personnel training programs, E-learning platform, on-the-job education, education promotion and job rotation, the School of Management contributes to the growth of employees.

Kingfa Employee Welfare Protection

Kingfa provides a highly competitive salary system and fixed income to guarantee the living standards and quality of life of employees. At the same time, the company has formulated a variety of incentive mechanisms, including technology marketing commission incentives, performance bonuses, year-end bonuses, standard cost incentives, project incentives, equity incentives, etc., greatly stimulating staff enthusiasm and creativity.
